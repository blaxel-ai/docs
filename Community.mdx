Blaxel has an active community of developers building AI agents, sandboxes, and MCP servers. Join us to ask questions, share what you're building, and get help directly from the Blaxel team.

The [Blaxel Discord](https://discord.gg/G3NqzUPcHP) is the best place to:

- Get real-time help from the Blaxel team and other developers
- Discuss best practices for building and deploying AI agents
- Stay up to date with new features and releases

You can also connect with Blaxel using social channels or email.
